Help

1.2012/03/02<FAQ>What is Brastel Remit?

Brastel Remit is a service where users can send money to friends and family overseas.
Different from remittance via banks, all procedure is available online.

2.2012/03/01<FAQ>Does Brastel Remit offer the same service as a bank?

No, Brastel Remit does not offer the exchange transactions conventionally provided by banks and other financial institutions. In accordance with the Fund Settlement Act (approved in April 2010), the international remittance service can also be offered by registered companies for remits below ¥1,000,000 (Brastel Remit Registration: Director General of Kanto Financial Bureau No. 00016).

3.2012/02/29<FAQ>Is there any subscription fee or annual membership cost?

No. Membership is free of charge and there are no additional fees to become a member, or to continue the membership.

4.2012/02/29<FAQ>Can I transfer money from overseas to Japan?

Currently the service is only available to transfer money from Japan to overseas.

5.2012/02/29<FAQ>Can the recipient of the remittance be a company/corporation?

Yes. Therefore, the recipient must be an individual that will represent as receiver for that company/corporation.

6.2012/02/29<FAQ>Can a corporation also use this service?

Yes, please access the “Procedure for Corporate Application” page for detailed information: brastelremit.jp/eng/corporate.

7.2012/02/29<FAQ>Is there any limit on the amount or times when making transfers with Brastel Remit?

Yes, there are daily and monthly limitations.
For detailed information, please go to: brastelremit.jp/eng/fees.

8.2012/02/29<FAQ>Can anyone apply for the service?

Anyone over 18 years old, residing in Japan, that has an email address, a Japanese bank account and ID verification documents recognized by the Japanese government may apply for the service. However, the company will hold the right to refuse applications after detailed analysis.

9.2012/02/29<FAQ>Can I use the Brastel Remit site from Mac OS?

There are no limitations on the operation system used.
We recommend accessing the website with an up-to-date browser.

10.2012/02/29<FAQ>Can I use the Brastel Remit site from a smartphone?

Basically there are limitations when accessing the Brastel Remit site from smartphones or tablets. However, depending on the device and OS used, some functions may not be available.

11.2012/02/29<FAQ>Is it safe to submit personal information to the Brastel Remit website?

Yes. All personal information you submit to us is encrypted and cannot be viewed by third parties.
On our secure pages, you will see that your browser’s address bar (or part of it) turns green; your browser may also show a lock icon.
This indicates that Brastel Co., Ltd. has been verified by a certificate authority as the owner of the site (to protect you from phishing), and that all the information you submit is encrypted before it is transmitted over the internet (which makes it difficult for others to view your information).

12.2012/02/29<FAQ>How soon can I use the service after applying?

After the application is accepted, we will send the member’s starter kit with information to activate your Brastel Remit account and start using the service. From the application review and starter kit delivery, a few days will be required.

13.2012/02/29<FAQ>Is the ID verification by delivery service Kuroneko Yamato available to any location?

Yes, as long as is within Japan’s territory. For more information about the delivery locations please access Kuroneko Yamato’s website or contact our customer service.

14.2012/02/29<FAQ>Are there any limits regarding the size of the image accepted for ID verification?

Yes. Our system will only accept files up to 2MB, in JPEG, PNG, GIF or PDF format.
If the image is blurry, or the stated information cannot be confirmed, we may request to send the ID copy by mail.

15.2012/02/29<FAQ>I’ve selected “home delivery” as delivery method. Is the delivery available to any area?

Even though “home delivery” is available in the country you are remitting to, depending on the address, the money must be collected at an agent collect point.
You may contact our customer service in advance to find out whether the delivery is available in the area you wish to send the money to.

16.2012/02/29<FAQ>How long will it take for the recipient to get the remittance?

Usually money is received within 1-3 business days, depending on the destination and delivery method, but you should allow a few more days if any additional information is required that would need further verification. If the money has not been delivered after a few days have passed, please contact customer service.

17.2012/02/29<FAQ>Will there be any charges for the recipient?

Depending on the country the recipient may be charged for withdraw fees, and local taxes may also apply.
Please contact the local authorities for detailed information.

18.2012/02/29<FAQ>How do I know the recipient received the money?

Currently we do not have a notification service to inform when the funds have been received.
When the money is available to be collected at the agent counter or home delivery, we will send you an email with the transaction Reference Number for you to inform the recipient to collect it.
In case of bank transfer, please ask the recipient to check his/her account balance.

19.2012/02/29<FAQ>What are the required documents the recipient will have to present to collect the money?

The recipient will be required to present their ID documents in addition to the reference number.

20.2012/02/28<FAQ>Where should I deposit the sending amount after placing the remittance request?

The money must be deposited in the account mentioned in the end of the remittance request (also sent by email).
Please note that bank transfer fee may apply. Also, the remittance request will be valid for one month.
If the deposit is not received until the end of the one-month period a new remittance request must be placed.

21.2012/02/28<FAQ>Is there a easier way to send money instead of having to place a remittance request online every time?

Brastel Remit offers the Easy Transfer option, where you may register up to 5 recipients and associate them to specific accounts for deposit to make transfers faster and easier.
By registering the recipients to the Easy Transfer option, you do not have to place a remittance request to send money to them; you simply deposit the sending amount into the associated account.
We will them deduct the transfer fee and send the subtotal to the recipient.

22.2012/02/28<FAQ>Can the Brastel Remit account be used to save or withdraw money like a normal bank account?

No, the Brastel Remit accounts are used specifically to receive the money you wish to send overseas and cannot be used as bank accounts.

23.2012/02/28<FAQ>What happens with the exchange rate if the remittance request is placed on a holiday?

The calculation will be based on the exchange rate of the last business day before the holiday.

24.2012/02/28<FAQ>What exchange rate is used when calculating the currency?

The remittance processed in Dollar-denominated Brastel rate’s, converted into the local currency.

25.2012/02/28<FAQ>At what point does the exchange rate apply? The final rate doesn’t match the one I saw when placing the request online.

The rate will be applied on the day that the amount is confirmed.
Please note that the exchange rate shown at the time the remittance request is placed is only for reference and may differ from the actual exchange rate.

26.2012/02/28<FAQ>What happens when my sending amount is different from my deposited amount?

After a remittance is processed, any remaining amount will be left in the account as a balance to be used in the next Standard Transfer.

27.2012/02/28<FAQ>Is Brastel Remit refundable?

Normally we do not cancel confirmed transactions, but refunds can be made in special circumstances. Please contact customer service for guidance on this matter.

28.2012/02/28<FAQ>Can I change the remittance amount of a request I’ve already submitted?

The content of a remittance cannot be changed after it has been processed.

29.2012/02/27<FAQ>Are there any special services for Brastel Card users?

Brastel Card users will get a special exchange rate discount.
To receive this rate, the Brastel Card must be registered under the user’s name and has to be recharged with a minimum of ¥2,000 in the last 3 months previous to the transfer date. Credits transferred from other Brastel Card are not considered as recharge.
To register your Brastel Card’s access code, please sign in to the Brastel Remit account.

30.2012/02/27<FAQ>Can I check which Brastel Card access code I have registered on the Brastel Remit service?

No. To check the registered access code you must contact customer service.

31.2012/02/27<FAQ>Can I use the credit from my Brastel Card to make a transfer via Brastel Remit?

No. The paid amount cannot be refunded or exchanged for cash. This is due to regulations regarding prepayment vouchers.

32.2012/02/27<FAQ>Can I share my username/account with friends and family?

No. The Brastel Remit account cannot be used by third parties.
Brastel will not be held accountable for any problem or damage that may arise as the result of any information shared with third parties.

33.2012/02/27<FAQ>Can I have more than one username/account?

No, we only allow one username/account per customer.

34.2012/02/27<FAQ>I’m having issues placing a request after changing my address or name. What should I do?

After changing your address/name, you need submit a copy of your ID documents where the new address/name can be verified. The same procedure also applies for company name change. Please contact customer service for detailed information.

35.2012/02/27<FAQ>I haven’t received the email with the authentication code from Brastel yet, what should I do?

Please double-check the email address you have entered.
If the address is correct, please make sure the email has not been blocked by your email filter or forwarded to your junk mailbox.

36.2012/02/27<FAQ>I’ve forgot my username/password. What should I do?

Go to the sign in page and click on the “Forgot your username or password?” link.
You will be requested to enter the email address registered to Brastel Remit and your date of birth to reset your account.
If you are not sure of the email address you originally registered, please contact customer service.

37.2012/02/27<FAQ>I can’t access my account because I’ve enter a wrong information too many times. What should I do?

Please contact customer service. Your account will be unblocked after identity confirmation.

38.2012/02/27<FAQ>How long will my account stay active if I don’t use the service for some time?

Your Brastel Remit account has a validation period of two years, this is automatically renewed unless you decide to terminate your
membership. The account will be terminated automatically if it has not been used in over two years. If you wish to continue the service after this period, a new application must be submitted.

39.2012/02/27<FAQ>Will I incur any penalties if I decide to terminate my membership before the two-year period has expired, and am I able to re-apply for the service?

No, the membership may be terminated at any time. After termination you may apply for the service again but it will be considered a new subscription.
