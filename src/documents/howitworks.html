How to proceed?

STEP 1

Become a member

Before starting the application process, please have the following at hand:

  • Email Address
  • ID verification documents
  • Bank account in Japan

Click on the "Subscribe Now" button and ﬁll in the application form. In a few days, you will receive a starter kit with information on how to activate your account.
The delivery time of the starter kit will depend on the ID veriﬁcation method you choose for the application process.

For corporate application, please click here.

ID Veriﬁcation Methods

As part of the application process for the Brastel Remit service, we must verify the applicant’s identity through ID documents. These documents can be submitted by different methods.

By image upload

Scan or take a picture of one of the ID documents indicated below and submit it together with your application. The ﬁle must be saved as JPEG, PNG, GIF or PDF format, and its size should not exceed 2MB.

  • Alien Registration Card (both sides)
  • Residence Card (both sides)
  • Driver's license (both sides)
  • Resident Registration Card (with photo)
  • Certiﬁcate of Residency (original, issued within the last three months)

You can also submit your passport (within validation, pages that contain your name, photo, passport number, expiration date and signature), insurance card or pension book PLUS a receipt of public utility charges stamped within the last three months.

Approx. delivery time of the starter kit: 2 - 4 days

By mail

After you submit your application, make a copy of one of the documents indicated below (with exception of the Certiﬁcate of Residency, which must be an original issued by the city hall) and send it by mail.

  • Copy of the Alien Registration Card (both sides)
  • Copy of the Residence Card (both sides)
  • Copy of the driver's license (both sides)
  • Copy of the Resident Registration Card (with photo)
  • Certiﬁcate of Residency (original, issued within the last three months)

You can also submit your passport (within validation, pages that contain your name, photo, passport number, expiration date and signature), insurance card or pension book PLUS a receipt of public utility charges stamped within the last three months.

Approx. delivery time of the starter kit: 4 - 5 days

By Kuroneko Yamato Delivery Service

After your application has been processed, a starter kit with the username and temporary password to access your account, will be delivered to you by Kuroneko’s restricted delivery service.
At the time of delivery, the deliveryman will request you to present one of the documents below.

  • Valid Passport
  • Alien Registration Card
  • Residence Card
  • Driver's License
  • Insurance Card
  • Pension Book

Approx. delivery time of the starter kit: 3 - 10 days

Face-to-face

You can also present your ID documents at one of Brastel Remit counters, or at events where our staff are providing on-site assistance.

  • Alien Registration Card
  • Residence Card
  • Driver's License
  • Insurance Card

You can also submit your passport (within validation, pages that contain your name, photo, passport number, expiration date and signature), insurance card or pension book PLUS a receipt of public utility charges stamped within the last three months.

The registration process will take approximately one hour to complete.

STEP 3

Make the deposit

After placing the request or creating the Easy Transfer account, you only need to deposit the amount you wish to send to the recipient.
Available deposit methods

Deposit method for Standard Transfers

The deposit for Standard Transfers must be made into the Mizuho Bank account you receive after registering for the service.

Deposit method for Easy Transfers

There are two deposit methods for the Easy Transfer accounts, which can be selected when registering each account (you can register up to ﬁve accounts).

When selecting Mizuho Bank, you will receive one Mizuho Bank account for each Easy Transfer you create. Any amount of money deposited to that account will be automatically calculated and sent to associated recipient.
Recommended option if you prefer to deposit from Mizuho and other banks using Internet banking, ATMs and at branches.

By choosing Japan Post Bank, a deposit card will be issued and associated to the Easy Transfer account you create. The amount of money you deposit using that deposit card will be automatically calculated and sent to associated recipient.
Unlike deposits to the Mizuho Bank account, the Japan Post Bank deposit cards can only be used at Japan Post Bank ATMs and branches, and is available only in cash. Deposits using other methods are not available.

DONE!

As soon as the deposit is confirmed, the process is finalized and the money sent to the recipient.

The money will be available to the recipient according to the chosen delivery method:

  • Bank account deposit: between 1 and 3 business days
  • Agent Collect Point: averagely 1 hour
  • Home delivery: between 1 and 2 business days

(It may take longer depending on holidays, in Japan and at the destination.)

TWO TRANSFER METHODS

Brastel Remit offers two methods of transfer request to best suit your needs.

Easy Transfer

Easy Transfer is the ideal method if you will be sending funds regularly to a specific recipient. You may associate the recipient to an exclusive bank account for deposits, allowing faster transfers without the need of placing a request every time.
Any amount you deposit to the account will be automatically processed and sent to the associated recipient. With Easy Transfer you may register up to 5 recipients.
